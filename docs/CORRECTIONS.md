## 2026-07-04: Mawile-GX and Crispin notation

The label `turbo/-Mawile+Crispin` means a **decklist mutation only**:

```text
Remove 1 Mawile-GX
Add 1 Crispin
```

It does not describe a connector edge, play sequence, search line, or Ability interaction. Mawile-GX has no route to Crispin.

Mawile-GX's Captivating Wink only interacts with the opponent's hand and Bench when Mawile-GX is played from hand to the Bench. It neither searches the player's deck nor advances Regidrago's evolution, Energy, payload-discard, or Supporter-access axes. Mysterious Treasure also cannot find Mawile-GX because Mawile is Metal, and Quick Ball finding Mawile does not advance this setup model.

The swap result measures a narrow goldfish deck-composition change: one low-immediate-setup slot was replaced by a third copy of an Energy-acceleration Supporter. It does **not** establish that removing Mawile-GX is strategically correct in a real Mimikyu Copycat or forced-bench matchup. Mawile's anti-Copycat value remains outside the first-Apex setup objective.

All future documents use `remove X; add Y` for deck mutations. `A -> B` is reserved for an actual legal game-state connector or action sequence.
